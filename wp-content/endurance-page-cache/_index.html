LMS Evaluation Tool

Use this evaluation tool to review a learning management system based on the information and demonstration provided by the vendor.

Evaluate each rubric by using the dropdown menus to select one of the following values:

  • 0 (Feature Not Present)
  • 5 (Fair)
  • 8 (Good)
  • 10 (Excellent)

This tool covers 27 rubrics, and automatically calculates and displays the point total at the bottom of the page. If you’d like to receive a copy of your evaluation results, you can enter your email address and click the Submit button. You will receive a copy of the form entries in the body of the email, as well as a CSV file for easy importing into your favorite spreadsheet software.

If you have any questions or suggestions for this tool, contact me. I hope you find it useful.

This tool is based on the LMS Evaluation form provided by North Carolina Agricultural and Technical State University.
